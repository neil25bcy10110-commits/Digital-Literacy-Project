**Social Media Responsibility Checklist for College Students**

**Do's**

* Think before you post; ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.  
* Protect your privacy by using strong passwords and adjusting privacy settings.  
* Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.  
* Maintain a positive digital footprint that reflects your personality and values.  
* Be respectful and polite in online discussions and comments.  
    
  


**Don'ts**

* Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly (e.g., address, phone number).  
* Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.  
* Do not engage in cyberbullying or trolling.  
* Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown files.  
* Do not overshare details that could harm your reputation or safety.
